our backs


tell stories


no books


have the spine to


carry

· · ·


let it go


let it leave


let it happen


nothing in this world


was promised or


belonged to you


anyway

· · ·


your body


is a museum


of natural disasters

· · ·


give to those


who have nothing


to give to you

· · ·

Poems by Rupi Kaur
